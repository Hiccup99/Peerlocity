About Us

"Data on the go"




Peerloctiy, in collaboration with Bitmark, allows individuals to register their bikes in a blockchain system that records and stores robust telemetry data about the bikes's use.

This system allows bikes to become an active, fluid, and secure resource within the larger transportation ecosystem.

  • Much needed data produced by Bikes are stored.
  • Bike data is analyzed by planners and researchers to design and reinvigorate our cities with smart and sustainable principals.
  • Data producers and users are reimbursed in the form of other transportation credits, BART, MUNI, etc.
